STANDING WHEELCHAIR

About

The purpose of this project is to conceive an automated mechanical wheelchair that embodies the pinnacle of engineering ingenuity, with an unwavering emphasis on the paramount tenets of safety, mechanism functionality, wheelchair functionality, and geometry. Our objective is to engineer a standing wheelchair that is imbued with a level of sophistication that exceeds all expectations, resulting in an unrivaled experience for the user.

Our project aims to assist individuals who are currently facing temporary impairments in their mobility and are in the process of recovering. Our goal is to create an innovative mechanical wheelchair that will serve as a tool to aid in their rehabilitation and facilitate a smoother and more comfortable transition towards improved physicalÂ autonomy.
